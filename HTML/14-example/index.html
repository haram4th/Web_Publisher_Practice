Lets keep it simple. We create beautiful, user friendly digital solutions. Founded in 1998, Enlighten Designs was built on a passion for people and has a vision to bring exceptionally designed, innovative and custom focused technology solutions to the world from the shores of New Zealand. We have seen the industry change, grow and flourish for over two decades and we’re still here - proof that we’ve got to be doing something right.

What makes us stand out in the ever-growing crowd of software companies? We listen. We believe that your solution needs to be as unique as your business. We take the time to learn about you, understand your goals, your vision and what you want to achieve. Then we help you get there – from the beginning, to delivery and beyond.
